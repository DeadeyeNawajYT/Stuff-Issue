Our Story

We Fell in Love With Giving Products a Soul

It started with a simple question: what if skincare products could feel? What if that little serum bottle on your shelf had feelings, dreams, and a desire to be part of your story?

That idea became Stuff Issue. We create 3D animated characters out of everyday skincare products — giving them eyes, hands, legs, and most importantly, emotions. Each one wears the signature Stuff Issue sky blue shorts. Because when a face cream character wakes up excited just to be part of your morning, you don't forget that.

Today, we help skincare brands find the emotional soul hidden inside their bottles. People don't fall in love with ingredients. They fall in love with feelings. And feelings are what we animate.

"Every skincare product has a character hiding inside it — a personality, a story, a reason someone reaches for it at the end of a long day. Our job is to bring that character to life so honestly that people smile, share, and never forget it."

Why Animated Storytelling

Ads interrupt. Characters invite.

The best skincare brands don't sell routines — they sell the way you want to feel. We help you translate that feeling into animated characters people can't scroll past.
